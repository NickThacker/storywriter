Store selections via `addCharacter` / `removeCharacter` on the store.

**`setting-step.tsx`:**
Header: "Where does your story take place?" Renders `<CardPicker options={SETTINGS} selected={setting} onSelect={setSetting} />`. Single-select.

**`tone-step.tsx`:**
Combined step for tone, beat sheet, and target length (grouped as "Style & Structure"):
